Vanilla Chia Pudding

Yummy, this is how you do it

This is quite delicious. The only issue I had was the chia seeds clumped together. I suggest mixing it periodically to separate the seeds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • â…” cup chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine milk, chia seeds, vanilla extract, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  3. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight.
